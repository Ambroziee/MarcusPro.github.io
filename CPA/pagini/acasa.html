What is CPA marketing?

CPA stands for advertising marketing model where publishers are paid for the action following their marketing activity. This differs from typical affiliate marketing where you don't necessarily have to sell to earn within a CPA network.

Payments are usually based on generating leads such as:

  • Submitting a phone number
  • Creating an account
  • Submitting a email address
  • Taking a short survey
  • Entering your details for a free trial

Why you should choose CPA?

Low-cost business idea!

It's a fairly easy industry to break into, namely because there are no barriers at first.

All you need to do is create an affiliate marketing website (but not required) or social media account, choose a compelling niche and a product or service that is relevant to you to market.

No expertise required

You don't have to be a marketing expert to start working as an affiliate.

The great thing about affiliate marketing is that you can learn on the job by testing different methods of promotion.

Low investment costs

In the beginning, CPA affiliate marketing expenses are almost zero.

You work online so you can do it from home pretty well and then you only have the basic expenses of electricity and internet connection.

Optionally, there are additional costs related to creating a website and also some minor costs related to layout design
